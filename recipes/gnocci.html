Recipe Description

Light and fluffy gnocchi are served in an aromatic, basil pesto in this simple summer dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 4 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 5-6 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Â½ cup high-quality extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. If using a food processor or blender, add all the ingredients and blend until green paste forms and there are no longer any large leaves or chunks of cheese.
  2. First, add the garlic and pine nuts. Grind until the consistency of shortbread dough.
  3. Next, add the basil leaves and salt. Crush in a circular motion with a pestle until you have a green paste.
  4. Add in the garlic paste to the crushed basil and grind together.
  5. Finally, add in the grated cheese and extra virgin olive oil. Drizzling the olive oil in last will help clean off any bits of pesto on your pestle and the sides of your mortar.
